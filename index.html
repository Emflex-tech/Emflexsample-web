Charge Gadgets While You Move

EMFLEX is a belt bag that converts motion into electricity using electromagnetic induction.

About EMFLEX

EMFLEX is a wearable power generator designed for people on the move. As the user walks or runs, motion inside the bag moves magnets through coils of wire. This movement generates electricity that can charge small devices such as phones, earbuds, and smart watches.

Key Features

  • ⚡ Motion powered electricity
  • ⚡ Portable gadget charging
  • ⚡ Lightweight belt bag design
  • ⚡ Uses electromagnetic induction

Faraday's Law Visual Explanation

Faraday's Law states that moving a magnet through a coil of wire produces an electric current.
